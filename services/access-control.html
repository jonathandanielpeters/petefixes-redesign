Access Control

Smart access solutions that let you manage who enters your property and when. From simple keypads to full networked systems, we design and install access control for homes and businesses.

Residential Home Entry Systems
Commercial Multi-Tenant Solutions
Remote Smartphone Control
Integrated Gate + Fence + Access

Keypad Entry

Simple, reliable
code-based access.

Keypad entry systems provide straightforward, code-based access to your property. Assign unique codes to family members, employees, delivery drivers and contractors — and revoke them instantly when needed.

Weatherproof keypads rated for Winnipeg winters, backlit for night use, and compatible with all major gate operator brands.

  • Programmable user codes
  • Weatherproof & backlit
  • Temporary & one-time codes
  • Compatible with all gate operators

Card & Fob Readers

Hands-free
secure entry.

Proximity card and key fob readers offer fast, hands-free access without codes to remember. Simply tap or wave your credential and the gate opens. Perfect for multi-user environments where individual access tracking is important.

Each credential can be individually programmed, tracked and deactivated — giving you complete control over who accesses your property and when.

  • Proximity & RFID technology
  • Individual user tracking
  • Instant credential deactivation
  • Multi-door / multi-gate support
Get a Free Quote

Video Intercom

See who's there.
Open from anywhere.

Video intercom systems let you see, hear and speak with visitors at your gate from inside your home, office, or anywhere via your smartphone. Grant or deny access with a single tap — no need to walk to the gate.

HD cameras with night vision, two-way audio, and cloud recording options give you complete visibility and control over every visitor interaction.

  • HD video with night vision
  • Two-way audio communication
  • Smartphone app control
  • Cloud recording & visitor logs
Get a Free Quote

Smart Access

Your phone is
the key.

Smartphone-based access control systems eliminate the need for physical credentials entirely. Open your gate via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or cellular connection from anywhere in the world.

Set schedules, grant temporary access to guests, monitor entry logs in real time, and receive notifications every time your gate opens. The most flexible access control solution available.

  • Bluetooth, Wi-Fi & cellular
  • Scheduled & temporary access
  • Real-time push notifications
  • Multi-property management
Get a Free Quote

Multi-Tenant

One system.
Many users.

Multi-tenant access control systems are designed for condominiums, apartment buildings, office complexes, and shared commercial spaces. Each tenant gets their own access credentials and directory listing.

Visitors can look up tenants by name, call through the intercom, and tenants grant access remotely. Full audit trails and management dashboards give property managers complete oversight.

  • Tenant directory & search
  • Individual tenant credentials
  • Property manager dashboard
  • Visitor logs & audit trails
