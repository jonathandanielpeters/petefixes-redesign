Chain Link

Starting from $26/ft installed — ideal for property boundaries, dog containment, and yards where budget matters most.

Residential

Wood — Hybrid Build

Every wood fence we build uses driven metal posts — never wood posts in concrete. The result is a fence that looks like traditional wood but resists frost heave, rot, and leaning for 30+ years in Manitoba's climate.

Choose Postmaster hidden posts for a classic all-wood look, or black powder-coated 2x2 steel posts for a modern architectural contrast. Vertical, horizontal, board-on-board and custom patterns available.

  • Driven metal posts — no concrete
  • Pressure-treated or cedar boards
  • Vertical, horizontal & board-on-board
  • Postmaster or 2x2 steel post options

Pool-code compliant options available for properties with pools or hot tubs. 30+ year lifespan with virtually zero maintenance.

Residential

Vinyl Fencing

Zero-maintenance privacy fencing that never needs staining, painting, or sealing. We install vinyl exclusively on driven metal posts — no wood post option — so your vinyl fence won't shift, lean, or fail at the post like traditional vinyl installs.

Privacy, semi-privacy, and picket styles in white, tan, grey, and wood-grain finishes. Through-body colour means no chipping, peeling, or fading.

  • Metal posts only — no rot, no heave
  • Never needs staining or painting
  • Privacy, semi-privacy & picket styles
  • Multiple colours & wood-grain options
Learn More

Residential

Welded Wire

Clean, modern, and nearly invisible. Welded wire fencing blends into your landscape while providing reliable boundary definition and pet containment. Perfect for properties with views you don't want to obstruct.

Available in various mesh sizes and coatings. A versatile option for gardens, acreages, and properties that need a fence without the visual weight of solid panels.

  • Near-invisible sightlines
  • Various mesh sizes & gauges
  • Galvanized & PVC-coated
  • Pet & garden containment
