Gate Automation

The engine behind
every gate we install.

No matter which gate style you choose — driveway, estate, or a back lane opening — the operator is what makes it move. For new installs we work with LiftMaster and Genie — both offer smart home integration, solar-charged battery backup, cellular monitoring, and full access control integration. We service every major brand on the market, including legacy FAAC, Nice, DoorKing, HySecurity, Linear and Chamberlain systems.

See the complete operator comparison, feature matrix, and selection guide on our dedicated Gate Automation page.

  • Operator selection by gate weight, length & cycles
  • Smart app control (LiftMaster myQ, Genie Aladdin Connect)
  • Solar, battery backup & cellular monitoring
  • Service, repair & annual maintenance on every major brand
