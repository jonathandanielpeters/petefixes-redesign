Commercial Chain Link Fencing

Heavy-gauge, industrial-strength chain link fencing built for Winnipeg's toughest commercial and industrial applications. Rapid installation, built to last.

Heavy-Gauge Commercial Grade

Industrial strength
for demanding sites.

Commercial chain link fencing from Pete Fixes is a fundamentally different product than residential grade. We use 9-gauge and 6-gauge galvanized wire fabric, 2-3/8 inch and 4-inch OD steel posts, and concrete footings set well below Manitoba's frost line. The result is a perimeter that handles high winds, heavy snow loads and the daily rigours of industrial environments without sagging, leaning or failing.

Our commercial chain link is the go-to choice for construction sites, storage yards, equipment compounds, warehouses and manufacturing facilities across Winnipeg. With deeper post embedment and heavier framework than residential builds, these fences deliver the structural integrity that commercial properties demand. Available in galvanized silver, black, green and brown vinyl-coated finishes to suit any site.

  • 9-gauge and 6-gauge wire fabric options
  • 2-3/8" to 4" OD steel line and terminal posts
  • Concrete footings below Manitoba frost line
  • Galvanized and vinyl-coated finishes
  • Engineered for wind and snow loads

Heights & Top Options

Barbed wire, razor ribbon
& extended heights.

Commercial properties often require fence heights well beyond the standard 6 feet. Pete Fixes installs commercial chain link in heights from 8 to 12 feet, providing the elevated perimeter security that warehouses, construction sites and storage compounds need. Taller fencing is a proven deterrent, and combined with our top security options, it creates a formidable barrier against unauthorized entry.

We offer multiple top configurations to match your security requirements. Three-strand barbed wire arms angled outward at 45 degrees are the most common commercial option. For higher-security sites, we install razor ribbon coils or a combination of barbed wire and razor ribbon. All top security additions are installed using purpose-built arms and brackets that maintain a clean, professional appearance across the full run of fence.

  • 8, 10 and 12 foot fence heights
  • Three-strand barbed wire with 45-degree arms
  • Razor ribbon coil installations
  • Combined barbed wire and razor options
  • Straight, angled and Y-arm configurations
  • Professional, uniform appearance

Privacy Solutions

Slats, windscreen
& visual barriers.

Chain link does not have to mean full visibility. Pete Fixes offers a range of privacy solutions that transform standard chain link into an effective visual barrier. Aluminum or PVC privacy slats are woven vertically or diagonally through the mesh, providing up to 90% opacity while maintaining the structural benefits of chain link. Available in colours that complement your building and site aesthetics.

For temporary or seasonal privacy, we install commercial-grade windscreen fabric that attaches to the chain link with heavy-duty zip ties or aluminum grommets. Windscreen is ideal for construction sites that need visual screening, equipment yards where inventory should be hidden from view, and properties along high-traffic corridors. It also reduces wind exposure for workers and equipment within the fenced area.

  • PVC and aluminum privacy slats
  • Vertical and diagonal slat patterns
  • Commercial windscreen fabric
  • Up to 90% visual opacity
  • Wind reduction for interior areas

Gates & Access

Vehicle and pedestrian
access points.

A commercial chain link perimeter is only as effective as its gates. Pete Fixes installs single swing gates, double swing gates and cantilever slide gates sized for any vehicle — from standard cars to full tractor-trailers. Our commercial gates use the same heavy-gauge framework as the fence line, with industrial hinges, drop rods and heavy-duty latches that withstand years of daily use.

For properties that need controlled vehicle access, our slide gates can be paired with automatic gate operators, keypads, card readers and telephone entry systems. Pedestrian gates with panic hardware and self-closing hinges provide code-compliant emergency egress while maintaining perimeter security. Every gate installation includes proper bracing, tension adjustments and hardware alignment for smooth, reliable operation from day one.

  • Swing gates up to 24 feet wide
  • Cantilever slide gates for wide openings
  • Automatic operator compatibility
  • Pedestrian gates with panic hardware
  • Industrial hinges, latches and drop rods
  • Keypad, card reader and intercom ready
