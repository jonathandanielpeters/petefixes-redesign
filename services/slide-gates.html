Cantilever and v-track slide gates engineered for Winnipeg winters. Ideal for wide driveways, parking lots, loading docks and any entrance where a swing gate isn't practical.

Cantilever Slide Gates

Track-free. Snow-proof.
The Winnipeg standard.

Cantilever gates are the gold standard for Winnipeg because they never touch the ground. The gate hangs from counter-balanced rollers mounted to a rear support post, so snow, ice, mud and debris never interfere with operation. There's no ground track to shovel, no wheel to freeze, and no seasonal grief.

Perfect for driveways, parking lot entries, storage yards, and any opening between 10 ft and 40 ft. Built from galvanized steel frames that handle hurricane-force winds without sagging.

  • No ground track — nothing to freeze or shovel
  • Galvanized steel frames, 20+ year life
  • Openings from 10 ft to 40 ft
  • Chain link, welded wire or ornamental infill

V-Track Rolling Gates

When the budget
matters most.

V-track gates roll on a steel V-shaped track embedded in the driveway surface, guided by V-groove wheels on the gate frame. They're a lower-cost alternative to cantilever systems — ideal for protected commercial yards, fenced storage compounds, and warmer-climate installations.

V-track systems handle wider openings economically but require a clean track for reliable operation. We'll help you decide whether cantilever or v-track is the right fit for your site and climate conditions.

  • Lower upfront cost than cantilever
  • Supports very wide openings (40+ ft)
  • Ideal for sheltered commercial yards
  • Requires track maintenance in winter

High-Traffic Slide Gates

Built for hundreds
of cycles a day.

Standard slide gate hardware wears out fast when it's cycling 200+ times per day. Our high-traffic systems use industrial-grade operators, oversized truck assemblies, reinforced frames, and premium bearings designed for continuous-duty use.

Ideal for warehouse yards, distribution centres, high-security compounds, and gated communities. Every install includes optional integration with card readers, vehicle loops, surveillance cameras and license plate recognition.

  • Industrial continuous-duty operators
  • Reinforced frames and oversized rollers
  • High cycle-count rated components
  • Integrates with full access control systems
