Barrier Arm Gates

Fast vehicle access control for parking lots, condominiums, commercial buildings, and gated communities. Sub-second cycle times with full integration into card readers, ticket dispensers and license plate recognition.

Open Time 1–2 Seconds
Arm Lengths 8 ft–20 ft
Cycle Rating 2+ Million Cycles

Parking Lot Barriers

Manage traffic flow
without slowing it down.

Barrier arms are the standard for parking lot entry and exit because they open in seconds and tolerate hundreds of cycles per hour without failure. We install LiftMaster TECHNA and FAAC barrier systems with arms sized for 8 ft up to 20 ft openings.

Every install includes safety loops (to prevent the arm from dropping on a vehicle), a card reader or ticket dispenser, and optional integration with license plate recognition for hands-free entry.

  • LiftMaster TECHNA & FAAC barrier systems
  • 1–2 second open/close cycles
  • Safety loops & photo eye included
  • Card reader, ticket, or LPR integration

Condo & Community Entries

Resident-friendly
controlled access.

For condominiums, gated communities, and resident-only parking areas, barrier arms provide the right balance of control and convenience. Residents enter via fob, sticker transponder, LPR or smartphone credential — visitors use an intercom or phone-entry system.

We integrate residential barrier systems with UniFi Access, HID card systems, Liftmaster MyQ, and cloud-based management platforms so property managers can add, remove, and audit access from anywhere.

  • Resident fob, sticker, LPR or smartphone entry
  • Visitor intercom & phone-entry integration
  • UniFi Access, HID & cloud platform support
  • Remote management & audit trail

High-Security Barriers

When a barrier arm
isn't strong enough.

For secure industrial sites, data centres, logistics yards, and any property where a vehicle could plausibly ram a barrier arm, we install heavy-duty crash-rated bollard and wedge barriers alongside (or instead of) standard barriers.

Pair high-security vehicle barriers with LPR, biometrics, visitor management software and 24/7 surveillance for a hardened entry point that stops unauthorized vehicles in their tracks.

  • Crash-rated bollard & wedge barriers (optional)
  • Anti-tailgating & anti-ram protection
  • LPR, biometrics & visitor management
  • 24/7 surveillance integration
