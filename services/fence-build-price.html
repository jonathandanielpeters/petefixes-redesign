How to Use Build & Price

  1. Select a fence type from the panel on the left
  2. Click on the grid to place your first post
  3. Click again to place the second post — a fence line is drawn between them
  4. Keep clicking to add more posts and extend your fence
  5. Press Esc to finish a line, then start a new one anywhere
  6. Use the Gate tool to add gates between two posts

Tips: Scroll to zoom · Hold & drag to pan · Click a post to select & delete it
