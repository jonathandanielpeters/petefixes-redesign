Vehicle Detection

Reliable vehicle detection systems that trigger your gate automatically, keep traffic flowing, and meet safety compliance. From photo eyes to in-ground loops, we install and service every type of detection sensor.

Safety UL 325 Compliant
Residential Driveway Triggers
Commercial High-Traffic Loops
Year-Round Winnipeg Winter Rated

Above-Ground Sensors

Photo eyes, infrared
& radar detection.

Above-ground sensors provide fast, non-invasive vehicle and obstacle detection for automatic gates. Photo eyes use infrared beams to detect obstructions in the gate path — a critical safety requirement for every automatic gate installation.

Infrared motion sensors and radar/microwave detectors expand your detection zone beyond the gate opening, triggering the gate as vehicles approach and preventing closures while traffic is present. All sensors are rated for Winnipeg's extreme temperatures.

  • Photo eyes (infrared beam break)
  • Infrared motion detectors
  • Radar & microwave sensors
  • UL 325 gate safety compliance

In-Ground Detection

Loops & probes
buried beneath the surface.

Inductive loops are the most reliable method of vehicle detection — a wire loop is cut into the driveway surface and detects the metal mass of vehicles passing over it. Used worldwide at traffic signals, parking garages, and gated entries, loops provide consistent, weather-proof detection.

Driveway probes offer a simpler alternative — magnetic sensors installed in a small bored hole that detect vehicles by their magnetic signature. Ideal for residential driveways where cutting a full loop isn't practical.

  • Inductive loop detectors (saw-cut)
  • Driveway probes (magnetic)
  • Unaffected by snow, ice & rain
  • Free-exit & safety loop configurations

Smart Integration

Detection that connects
your entire system.

Vehicle detection is most powerful when it's part of a connected system. We integrate your sensors with gate operators, access control panels, surveillance cameras, and lighting — so your property responds intelligently to every vehicle that arrives or departs.

Approach detection triggers the gate before drivers stop. Safety sensors prevent gate closure on vehicles. Free-exit loops let vehicles leave without credentials. And all events are logged for security auditing and review.

  • Gate operator integration
  • Approach, safety & free-exit zones
  • Surveillance camera triggers
  • Event logging & audit trails
