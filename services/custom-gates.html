Bespoke Ornamental Gates

Artwork that
secures your entrance.

Our custom ornamental gates are welded, finished and installed in-house. From classic scrollwork and iron filigree to modern geometric panels, wildlife silhouettes, family monograms and contemporary laser-cut designs — every detail is yours to specify.

The design process starts with a site visit and concept sketches. Once the design is approved we fabricate, powder-coat and install. Typical lead time is 4–8 weeks depending on complexity.

  • Scrollwork, laser-cut, plasma-cut panels

Bi-Folding & Accordion Gates

Fit wide gates
into tight sites.

When you have a wide opening but no room for a swing gate to open and no run-off space for a slide gate, bi-folding and accordion gates are the answer. Each panel folds against itself and stacks neatly against the gate post, so the gate footprint when open is only a fraction of its closed span.

Bi-fold gates use two panels per side that fold in half — ideal for urban driveways and narrow side entries. Accordion gates use multiple linked panels that zig-zag compactly — perfect for very wide commercial entries and industrial openings where full clearance is required.

  • Bi-fold: ideal for urban driveways with tight clearance
  • Accordion: handles 20 ft+ openings, stacks compactly
  • Full-height security panels or ornamental infill
  • Powered by specialized folding gate operators

Privacy & Specialty Gates

Full-coverage panels
for complete privacy.

Privacy panel gates use sheet metal, wood slats, vinyl, composite, or louvered steel to block visibility through the gate — matching your privacy fence line and giving you a fully enclosed perimeter. Great for pool areas, commercial service yards, and homes backing onto busy streets.

We also fabricate specialty gates that don't fit any standard category: oversized industrial rollers, arched tops to match architectural details, curved gates to follow a driveway arc, and gates integrated into stone pillar structures.

  • Full-coverage privacy panels matched to fence
  • Oversized, arched and curved custom fabrications
  • Integration with masonry pillars and entry features
  • Matched design + materials for a cohesive look
