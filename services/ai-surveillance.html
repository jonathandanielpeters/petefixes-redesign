AI Analytics Cameras

Cameras that think,
not just record.

AI analytics cameras use deep learning to distinguish between people, vehicles, animals and environmental movement. Instead of flooding you with false alarms from blowing leaves or passing shadows, these cameras send targeted alerts only when genuine threats are detected.

Define intrusion zones, tripwire lines, and loitering thresholds on each camera. Receive instant push notifications with AI-classified snapshots — so you know exactly what triggered the alert before you even open the app.

  • Person & vehicle classification
  • Intrusion zone & tripwire alerts
  • Loitering & abandoned object detection
  • 95%+ false alarm reduction

License Plate Recognition

Your gate reads plates.
Opens for the right ones.

LPR cameras capture and read license plates in real time, matching them against your allowlist to trigger automatic gate opening — completely hands-free. No remotes, no cards, no codes. Just drive up and the gate opens for authorized vehicles.

Maintain allowlists and blocklists, log every vehicle entry with timestamped plate captures, and receive alerts for unknown plates. Ideal for gated communities, commercial yards, parking facilities, and high-security sites.

  • Automatic gate trigger by plate
  • Allowlist & blocklist management
  • Timestamped vehicle entry logs
  • Unknown plate alerts

Remote Monitoring

Every camera.
One dashboard. Anywhere.

Cloud and local NVR recording gives you continuous 24/7 coverage with instant playback. View live feeds from any camera on your smartphone, tablet or desktop — whether you're on-site, at home, or halfway around the world.

AI-powered event search lets you find specific incidents in seconds instead of scrubbing through hours of footage. Multi-site dashboards let you monitor multiple properties from a single app with unified alerts and recordings.

  • Cloud & local NVR recording
  • Smartphone & desktop live view
  • AI-powered event search
  • Multi-site unified dashboard
