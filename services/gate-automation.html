Swing Gate Operators

Match the operator
to the swing gate.

Every swing gate needs an operator sized to its weight, width, duty cycle and power source. For new installs we work with LiftMaster and Genie — both well-supported in Canada, with proven performance in Winnipeg winters. The right pick depends on the job, not the badge.

Slide Gate Operators

Horsepower matched
to gate weight.

Slide gate operators drive a chain, rack or belt to move the gate panel across the opening. LiftMaster and Genie both offer residential through heavy-commercial slide operators — we'll size the horsepower, duty cycle and drive type to the gate you actually have.

Operator Type Max Gate Weight Max Gate Length Cycles / Day Best For
LiftMaster SL585Solar ready Residential Slide (DC) 1,000 lb 50 ft 300 Residential — our default slide operator
LiftMaster SL3000 Residential Slide (AC) 1,000 lb 40 ft 100 Residential AC-powered alternative
LiftMaster CSL24ULCommercial Commercial Slide (DC) 2,500 lb 60 ft 1,500 Medium-duty commercial, UL 325
LiftMaster HCT24ULHigh Cycle Commercial Slide (DC) 5,000 lb 85 ft Unlimited High-traffic commercial, warehouses
Genie Residential SlideSolar ready Residential Slide (DC) 1,000 lb 40 ft 200 Residential driveways — Genie alternative
Genie Pro Commercial SlideCommercial Commercial Slide (DC) 2,500 lb 60 ft 1,500 Medium-duty commercial sites

Gate weight dictates horsepower. Always add 20–30% headroom for Manitoba winter ice load and solid panel wind load.

Barrier Arm Operators

Speed first.
Everything else second.

Barrier arms are all about cycle time. The operators are purpose-built for the high-frequency, short-distance motion of a parking gate — usually 1–2 seconds per cycle, hundreds of thousands of cycles per year, without maintenance. LiftMaster's MegaArm and TECHNA families cover most parkade and condo jobs; Genie offers commercial parking-gate operators for lighter-duty lanes where simpler controls and a lower price point fit the job.

Operator Open Time Max Arm Length Cycle Life Best For
LiftMaster MegaArm DC 2 sec 14 ft 1M cycles Condos, small parkades — DC entry model
LiftMaster MegaArm Pro 1.5 sec 14 ft 2M cycles Parking lots, condos, commercial
LiftMaster MegaArm Tower 1.5 sec 20 ft 2M cycles Wide openings, commercial yards
LiftMaster TECHNANewest 1 sec 20 ft 3M cycles Highest-traffic — fast-cycle spec
Genie Commercial Barrier 2 sec 14 ft 1M cycles Light-commercial parking, small parkades

LED-lit arms, articulating arms (for low-clearance garages), skirted arms, and breakaway arms are available on most LiftMaster models. Need crash-rated (K4/K8/M30/M50) or anti-ram hardware? That's outside the LiftMaster and Genie lineups — we specify and service those separately, ask us for options.

Power & Connectivity

Keep your gate running
when the power doesn't.

Every automatic gate we install includes battery backup rated for at least 10 cycles during a power outage. For sites without reliable 120V power we install solar-charged systems with 2+ days of battery autonomy even through a Winnipeg winter.

Connectivity matters too — cellular modems let you monitor and control the gate from anywhere, even if your home WiFi goes down. All modern operators support smartphone entry, remote codes, usage logging and instant alerts.

  • Battery backup: 10–100+ cycles depending on size
  • Solar charging: 2+ days autonomy year-round
  • Cellular LTE modem for remote monitoring
  • Smartphone entry & virtual codes
  • Event logging & instant alerts

Service & Repair

Operator won't open?
We'll get it running.

Even the best gate operators eventually need service. Common issues include frozen photo eyes, dead backup batteries, broken chains, burned-out control boards, and loose limit switches. We service every major brand on the market — not just the ones we install — so your existing operator stays covered whatever badge it wears.

If you're looking at your operator right now and it won't open, check our troubleshooting guide before you call — it'll often save you a service charge. If the problem is beyond DIY, we offer emergency service across Winnipeg.

  • Service on LiftMaster, Genie, Chamberlain, Nice / Apollo, HySecurity, FAAC, DoorKing, Linear, Elite, All-O-Matic, Viking, Ramset & more
  • Battery, photo eye, control board & chain replacement
  • Annual maintenance programs available
  • Emergency & after-hours service
