Slide Gate Operators

Horsepower matched
to gate weight.

Slide gate operators drive a chain, rack or belt to move the gate panel across the opening. LiftMaster covers most residential and commercial builds; for heavy industrial or crash-rated applications, HySecurity's hydraulic SlideDriver family is purpose-built for continuous-duty service.

Gate weight dictates horsepower. Always add 20–30% headroom for Manitoba winter ice load and solid panel wind load.

Barrier Arm Operators

Speed first.
Everything else second.

Barrier arms are all about cycle time. The operators are purpose-built for the high-frequency, short-distance motion of a parking gate — usually 1–2 seconds per cycle, hundreds of thousands of cycles per year, without maintenance. LiftMaster's MegaArm and TECHNA families cover most parkade and condo jobs; HySecurity StrongArm is our go-to for crash-rated and high-security lanes.

Power & Connectivity

Keep your gate running
when the power doesn't.

Every automatic gate we install includes battery backup rated for at least 10 cycles during a power outage. For sites without reliable 120V power we install solar-charged systems with 2+ days of battery autonomy even through a Winnipeg winter.

Connectivity matters too — cellular modems let you monitor and control the gate from anywhere, even if your home WiFi goes down. All modern operators support smartphone entry, remote codes, usage logging and instant alerts.

  • Battery backup: 10–100+ cycles depending on size
  • Solar charging: 2+ days autonomy year-round
  • Cellular LTE modem for remote monitoring
  • Smartphone entry & virtual codes
  • Event logging & instant alerts

Service & Repair

Operator won't open?
We'll get it running.

Even the best gate operators eventually need service. Common issues include frozen photo eyes, dead backup batteries, broken chains, burned-out control boards, and loose limit switches. We service all four major brands.

If you're looking at your operator right now and it won't open, check our troubleshooting guide before you call — it'll often save you a service charge. If the problem is beyond DIY, we offer emergency service across Winnipeg.

  • Service on LiftMaster, Nice & HySecurity (+ legacy FAAC / DoorKing)
  • Battery, photo eye, control board & chain replacement
  • Annual maintenance programs available
  • Emergency & after-hours service
