Chainlink Pricing

Master material list + build recipes. The fence drawing tool uses these recipes to calculate the parts needed for each post type, per foot of fence, and per gate type. Color variants (galvanized/black) are matched automatically to the customer's selection.

Chain Link Pricelist 2026 PARTS CATALOG
The full 444-SKU wholesale parts catalog. Every row is auto-classified into a target Material (e.g. "Brace Band", "Chain Link Fabric 2"KK 11ga", "Post Tubing 2-3/8"") and a Variant (size / height / length within that material). The Chain Link Recipes section sources its pickers directly from this list — edit a cost here and every recipe that references that SKU re-prices automatically.
⚙︎ Commercial Edition HEAVY-DUTY
Commercial pricing is a separate layer applied when the customer flips the Commercial toggle on the Build & Price tool. It uses its own materials, recipes, labour, and gates so heavy-duty work doesn't get residential prices. Items above tagged Commercial or Both are eligible — wire them into the recipes below.
When off, the toggle is hidden in the Build & Price tool. When on, customers see a Mode banner they can flip to commercial.
Next: we can add a dedicated Commercial recipe builder (separate from the residential recipes above) and a commercial-only gate pricing chart. The 2026 pricelist already has 199 commercial-tagged items + 67 dual-use items ready to wire in.
