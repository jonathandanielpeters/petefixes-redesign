Swing Gates

The classic driveway gate — elegant, effective, and affordable. Single-panel and bi-parting double gates in steel, aluminum, wood and ornamental designs. Ideal for driveways under 24 ft with minimal slope.

Opening Widths 8–24 ft
Configurations Single & Bi-Parting
Materials Steel, Aluminum, Wood, Iron
Swing Direction In, Out, or Either

Single Swing Gates

One panel.
Simple. Affordable.

A single swing gate covers one opening with one panel hinged at the side. It's the most cost-effective automatic gate option for driveways up to about 16 ft wide, and it's perfect for residential side entries, walk gates, and secondary vehicle access.

Single swing gates work with affordable LA-series arm operators and fit most residential budgets. Choose between inswing or outswing based on your driveway slope, traffic pattern and clearance.

  • Best for openings 8 ft to 16 ft wide
  • Lowest-cost automatic gate option
  • Inswing or outswing configuration
  • Compatible with LA-series arm operators

Double Swing Gates

Two panels meet
in the middle.

Double (bi-parting) swing gates use two panels that meet in the centre of the opening — each panel swings independently on its own hinged side post. This configuration handles wider openings up to 24 ft while keeping each individual panel a manageable weight.

Bi-parting gates are the classic estate look — they make a grand entrance and work beautifully with ornamental iron, wood panel, or sleek modern steel designs. Both panels automate with a dual-operator kit.

  • Best for openings 12 ft to 24 ft wide
  • Classic estate entrance aesthetic
  • Dual-operator automation kit
  • Ornamental, wood, aluminum or steel

Pedestrian & Walk Gates

Separate foot
from vehicle traffic.

A dedicated pedestrian gate alongside your driveway swing gate keeps foot traffic safe and prevents unnecessary cycling of the big gate. Available as manual self-closing gates or fully automatic for accessibility.

We match the pedestrian gate style to the driveway gate and fence line so everything looks intentional. Add keypad entry, card access or intercom for visitor management without ever opening the vehicle gate.

  • Matched to your fence & driveway gate
  • Manual self-closing or automated
  • Keypad, card reader or intercom options
  • ADA-compliant configurations available
